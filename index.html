Bald Eagle

Haliaeetus leucocephalus

Endangered

The bald eagle is a bird of prey found in North America. A sea eagle, it has two known subspecies and forms a species pair with the white-tailed eagle.

Scarlet Macaw

Ara macao

Vulnerable

The scarlet macaw is a large red, yellow, and blue South American parrot. It is a member of a large group of Neotropical parrots known as macaws.

South America

Emperor Penguin

Aptenodytes forsteri

Near Threatened

The emperor penguin is the tallest and heaviest of all living penguin species and is endemic to Antarctica.
